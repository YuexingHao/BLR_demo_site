An 82 year old Caucasian man lives alone and who usually only sees his doctor for his annual physical. Before retirement, he was continuously employed in well paying jobs, and received a master’s degree. He lives alone, separated from partner many years ago. Has a good group of friends at a senior’s club. Review of his chart indicates well controlled hypertension and type II diabetes. He has no substance use history. He does not have any formal psychiatric diagnoses, but notes having felt ‘sad’ for periods previously.
